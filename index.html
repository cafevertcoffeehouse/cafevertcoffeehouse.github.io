Welcome

Development on Cafe Vert is paused while we wait for Massachussetts to pass legislation on social consumption

Welcome to Café Vert, an innovative coffeehouse experience in Easthampton, Massachusetts. Café Vert (from French, meaning "green coffeehouse"), is an inviting, multi-use coffeehouse where adults may gather to socialize and exchange ideas about the science, politics, culture, and history of cannabis and hemp. Massachusetts will make history when it launches its recreational marijuana market in July 2018. Now, more than ever, we need to have a trusted, comfortable space to facilitate fact-based learning and honest dialogue surrounding cannabis. Café Vert will achieve this goal by providing high quality education, rich programming, notable industry speakers, and a variety of classes in a safe, stigma-free environment. We invite you to enjoy a cup of freshly brewed hemp CBD Sträva Craft Coffee and sample some delicious, locally-sourced food during your visit. Café Vert is also a safe place for our patrons to just \"be\" and contemplate self-care.

We welcome all adults (age 21+).
